Instagram

A photo and video sharing app, Instagram has been a popular app throughout the years. You can share your lifestyle with friends and followers where they can view, comment, and like your posts. It is one of the best ways to social network your business and get public exposure.

Tiktok

Tiktok is a social media app with the intent of people of all ages to share and discover short videos. Used as an outlet for people to express themselves in many ways such as singing, dancing, comedy, and lip-syncing. It gives users the chance to find themselves and a community they best fit in.

X(Twitter)

A platform where users can express their thoughts in short messages, X ( formally known as Twitter ), is used by politicians, news outlets, celebrities, and average people. X gives users the space to share ideas, funny videos, polls, animated GIFs , and much more. Boasting 100 million users who are active on a daily basis, and with 500 million tweets sent out each day. The platform can be utilized for receiving news, keeping up with well-known personalities, or reconnecting with former classmates.

The Positives and Negatives

Positives

Social media is beneficial in many ways such as helping form connections, a way to express yourself,and a good way to market yourself.

Helps form relationship connections

The internet allows the ability to gain online connections by separating geographical barriers and allowing people to interact and communicate with people all around the world. Through social media platforms, forums, and online communities, individuals can connect with others who share similar interests, hobbies, or professional pursuits. The internet enables people to build relationships through messaging, video calls, and online gaming, creating a sense of community and belonging. It provides access to diverse perspectives and cultures, allowing individuals to engage with people from around the world and form meaningful connections based on shared values and experiences. The internet serves as a powerful tool for people to establish and nurture online connections with others.

Allows people to freely express themselves

Social media provides a platform for individuals to freely express themselves in various ways and gives them the ability to be yourself online without always having to show yourself in fear of being judged or shamed by others.. Users can share their thoughts, opinions, and experiences through written posts, photos, videos, and other forms. They can engage in discussions on a wide range of topics, connect with like-minded individuals, and participate in online communities. Additionally, social media allows people to showcase their creativity, talents, and projects, whether it's art, music, photography, or other forms of self-expression. It enables individuals to share their voice with the world and give them a sense of belonging and confidence they may not have had before.

Great marketing tactic

Social media platforms provide young entrepreneurs with a powerful tool to market their products to a wide audience. By using platforms such as Instagram, Tiktok, and Twitter, entrepreneurs can showcase their products and talents through photos, videos, and posts. With many teenagers starting their own brands whether it's braiding, nails, clothing, etc, social media allow them to interact directly with their target audience, receive feedback, and build a community around their brand. Additionally, social media allows entrepreneurs to run targeted ad campaigns to reach potential customers based on demographics, interests, and online behavior. Social media provides a cost-effective and efficient way for young entrepreneurs to establish and grow their businesses.

Negatives

Social media is beneficial yet is also damaging and has many down sides such as self-esteem issues, cyberbullying, and ptomoting social isolation.

Self-Esteem Issues

With the internet plastering an unrealistic beauty standard in the faces of many young girls, this has created a not so unexpected problem with self-esteem issues. This is a root of insecurities and body dysmorphia as people start comparing themselves and their lifestyle to others. Many high school students have spoken about the impact it has had on their mental wellbeing.

Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying. A form of harassment or aggression that takes place over social media platforms, text messages, basically on digital devices. It is often used to intimidate, threaten, or humiliate someone. Cyberbullying can have a significant impact on teenagers, causing emotional distress, anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem. The constant exposure to negative or hurtful messages can lead to feelings of isolation and can affect academic performance and overall well-being. Cyberbullying can result in teenagers experiencing increased stress and a reluctance to engage in social activities, both online and offline.

Promotes Social Isolation

Research has shown that there is a link between heavy social media use and social isolation and loneliness. When talking to people in person you may not know how to hold a conversation or may feel awkward. You may tend to ignore people as your phone has all of your attention.
